Astro Olympiad
The Astro Olympiad Round 1 is a competition organized by the British Physics Olympiad organisation. It specializes in astrophysical phenomena, using terms and equations rarely used in other fields of physics. It is supplemental to A Level physics as calculations and methods used are similar, but it introduces a much more in depth understanding of astronomy and enhances students’ problem solving skills.

Astro Round 1 2025

2025 is the first year where Runnymede students took the Astro Olympiad Round 1. Following the provided syllabus, the candidates self studied the further knowledge required and discussed their findings weekly with each other. Despite the limited time frame, between the three candidates, one … award and two commendation awards were achieved.
