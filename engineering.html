Runnymede STEM Club
engineering club
The Engineering Club was founded in October 2023 to promote green energies, foster creativeness, and fight female underrepresentation in the engineering field. At present, we are a group of 10 enthusiastic Sixth form students, with a passion for engineering. Amongst other projects, the one that we have focused on is creating sustainable solar power iPad chargers. Stay updated with our weekly progress with this website!

19/11/2024

We soldered the wires and connected the circuit. After leaving the glue to dry for 30 minutes, we tried out the solar panel. Because it was a cloudy day, we used a solar LED light instead of leaving it under the Sun. It powered a buzzer and lit up a blue LED light!


12/11/2024

We've officially started building our solar panel! This week we focused on arranging the solar panels on the cardboard, ensuring that they were in the correct orientation. By connecting them positive to negative, it allowed us to create a circuit for current to flow through.


5/11/2024

This week, we gathered the different components we needed: cardboard, wires, solar panels, solders. By doing research online, we came up with the instructions to create the solar panel. We created a presentation to put our different ideas together.
