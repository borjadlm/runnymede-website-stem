Runnymede STEM Club
Engineering Club
The Engineering Club was founded in October 2023 to promote green energies, foster creativity, and fight female underrepresentation in the engineering field. At present, we are a group of 10 enthusiastic Sixth Form students with a passion for engineering. Our main project is creating sustainable solar power iPad chargers. Stay updated with our weekly progress through this website!

19/11/2024

We soldered the wires and connected the circuit. After leaving the glue to dry for 30 minutes, we tried out the solar panel. Since it was a cloudy day, we used a solar LED light instead of the Sun. It powered a buzzer and lit up a blue LED light!


12/11/2024

We've officially started building our solar panel! This week, we focused on arranging the solar panels on the cardboard, ensuring they were in the correct orientation. By connecting them positive to negative, we created a circuit for current to flow through.


5/11/2024

This week, we gathered the different components: cardboard, wires, solar panels, and solders. After researching online, we compiled instructions to create the solar panel and created a presentation to organize our ideas.
