Runnymede STEM Club
engineering club
The Engineering Club was founded in October 2023 to promote green energies, foster creativeness, and fight female underrepresentation in the engineering field. At present, we are a group of 10 enthusiastic Sixth form students, with a passion for engineering. Amongst other projects, the one that we have focused on is creating sustainable solar power iPad chargers. Stay updated with our weekly progress with this website!
